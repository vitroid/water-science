Alternative Tetrahedral Water Clusters

Connectivity maps of some small optimized tetrahedral water clusters [429] are shown below. Compare these with the icosahedral (512) water cluster connectivity map (Schlegel diagram) (with 30,026 symmetry independent hydrogen-bonded configurations). Larger clusters based on, for example, tetrakaidecahedral (51262) (with 3,043,836 symmetry independent hydrogen-bonded configurations), pentakaidecahedral (51263), and hexakaidecahedral (51264) (with 61,753,344 symmetry independent hydrogen-bonded configurations) are also possible.
